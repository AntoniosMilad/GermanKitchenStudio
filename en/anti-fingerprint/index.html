    Anti-fingerprint coating

    Our anti-fingerprint fronts are finished with an innovative anti-fingerprint coating. This coating makes the surface particularly robust, insensitive and more resistant to annoying fingerprints. Absolutely suitable for everyday use!

    Velvety and highly honed surfaces

    The anti-fingerprint front offers an extremely matt surface thanks to UV radiation. This gives this lacquered front a particularly soft and pleasant feel.

    Intense colours
