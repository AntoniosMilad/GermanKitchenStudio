    Kitchen highlights

    Kitchens are always at the centre of life: the heart of the home, a meeting place, a stage for life’s dramas or private round the clock restaurant. And because kitchens have always been a matter of taste, our kitchens can adapt to suit the demands of your everyday life. Individuality with a plan – that was and remains the idea behind our furniture, which is expanded every year to incorporate new designs and functional solutions.     Anti-fingerprint front:
    EASYTOUCH

    If you like a puristic look, you’re sure to love our high-quality Easytouch surface. An ultra-matt appearance not only looks fantastic but also hits the mark with its practical anti-fingerprint properties. The innovative surface coating finish on the trendy front means fewer annoying finger smudges. The right shade for every taste: in addition to the Mineral green décor, Easytouch is also available in another five attractive colours. For wood décor fans, there’s also our NATURA in Oak Montreal with an anti-fingerprint surface.

    FURTHER INFO

    Ceramic worktop:
    Xtra Ceramic

    A real eye-catcher! The worktop with a real ceramic surface in matt Black boasts an innovative sandwich structure and is an elegant highlight for any kitchen. In addition to its good looks, the worktop is especially robust and therefore prepared for all the challenges of everyday life. Perhaps you’re not a big fan of Black? No problem! The Xtra Ceramic worktop is available in five attractive décors. See for yourself – and discover the other benefits of Xtra Ceramic in the process!

    FURTHER INFO

    Exclusive Sally oven from BOSCH with Home Connect

    Our range of electric appliances for the kitchen exclusively includes the Sally oven from Bosch, which comes equipped with state-of-the-art Home Connect technology. This enables you to adjust the settings on the appliance, switch it on and off and check the run time via smartphone or tablet, whenever suits you. To ensure maximum convenience, this oven is also integrated at an ergonomic height and blends seamlessly into the Mineral green design from our Easytouch range.⁠ Take advantage of the other highlights of this innovative oven and learn about the recipe app to which it can be easily connected

    BORA GP4 downdraft extractor fan

    The unique cooperation with BORA offers innovative high-end extractor systems for our kitchens. Exclusively from us: the BORA GP4 hot plate with integrated extractor. The unit solution tailor-made specifically for this leaves plenty of storage space below the hot plate. And there’s no need to cry over spilled milk – or any other spills: if a part ever gets dirty, it’s easy to remove and dishwasher-safe. In addition to all that, the BORA GP4 has a lot more to offer. See for yourself!

    FURTHer Info

    Storage space solutions

    nobilia offers attractive storage space solutions for your kitchen – whether you want to use the complete interior of a drawer in the base unit underneath the BORA GP4 hot plate, a practical CARGO pantry unit with lots of space for supplies or the JUNIOR larder pull-out for a narrow corner of a room. This maximises the usefulness of the available space and ensures you always have all important kitchen utensils within easy reach.

    Order with a system

    Always a neat feeling! A utility room is the ultimate space for anyone who loves a well-organised home. The idea of a pantry off your kitchen in particular is making a contemporary comeback. The secret helpers working in the background: flexible cupboards and shelves that offer lots of customisable storage space for almost everything you can think of.

    Practical access

    A pantry off a kitchen is integrated perfectly into the design to minimise legwork. But we’ve saved the best for last: simply shut the door and enjoy a complete lack of clutter. All thanks to the practical access door solution in cabinet look.
