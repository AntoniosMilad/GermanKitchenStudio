    We help you with planning your fitted kitchen.

    There are many points that must be considered when planning a kitchen: Which household appliances should be built in, how much room is available and what should the dream kitchen look like later? nobilia makes planning the kitchen easy for you: with helpful tools such as the checklist, the online planner, and our kitchen configurator.

    Checklist for kitchen planning

    Before you meet with a kitchen expert at a specialised retailer for a comprehensive consultation, you should be clear on several important points. Answer the most important planning questions in advance using the following checklist. Simply fill in the checklist together with your family, print it out and use it as a basis for discussion when purchasing your kitchen.
